Our Story

On Monday 5th August 2019, Hayden and Natalie first spoke having 'swiped right' on each other on the infamous dating app Tinder. In less than 24 hours, Hayden swiftly (and cheekily) gave Natalie his number and a first date was set soon after.

On Tuesday 13th of August, Hayden and Natalie saw each other for the first time at the most romantic of places - Cosham Train Station. From there, they proceeded onwards to Playzone (a children's soft play area holding an adult evening) where they spent many hours running around acting like big children. After a few double unicorn vodka slushies (and one awful toffee vodka drink), they made their way back to Cosham train station and had their first kiss before departing ways just before midnight.

Less than a day later, Hayden asked Natalie out on a second date. Many others followed and Hayden officially asked Natalie to be his girlfriend on the 8th of September to which she eagerly responded yes.

In the following months, Hayden and Natalie went on many adventures including their first trip (and only) abroad together. When the first lockdown was announced on the 23rd March 2020, Hayden decided to move in with Natalie so they could be together every day. Lots of baking and a few walks happened in those months and in June 2020, they moved in together to their own rented property in Portsmouth.

On the 18th December 2021, Hayden proposed to Natalie in their living room using some puzzle post (a mini escape room which Hayden has gotten Natalie every Christmas). They shared their good news with their families at the Pompey Panto that evening and with friends soon after. Wedding planning began almost immediately.

After the proposal, Hayden and Natalie continued their life adventures together including purchasing their first house together in Fareham where they now live. They picked up the keys on the 21st June and have since begun making the house their own.

Hayden and Natalie are looking forward to sharing their special day with all of you and are looking forward to all the future adventures and making loads of new memories together.

Itinerary

The Ceremony 12:30 - 13:30

Please arrive promptly at 12:30. At this time, you will be directed towards the Greenside barn where you can get yourself a drink before the ceremony which will take place in the Greenwood room. The wedding ceremony will begin at 13:00 and finish at approximately 13:30.

Post ceremony 13:30 - 15:00

After the ceremony, welcome drinks will be served downstairs in the Garden room. Our welcome drinks are piña coladas (non-alcoholic versions available). A fully stocked bar will also be on hand if piña coladas aren't your thing. The bride and groom will gladly accept any spare piña coladas. Group photographs will be taken during this time slot so listen out for instructions from the teachers in attendance! This part of the day will also include a few surprises which will remain a secret until the big day.

Wedding breakfast and speeches 15:00 - 17:15

At 3pm, you will be invited to return to the Greenwood room where the wedding breakfast will be served. A fully stocked bar upstairs will be open throughout so please feel free to buy drinks at any time throughout the meal. A starter and main will be served and this will be followed by speeches from both the grooms party and the bridal party.

Post wedding breakfast 17:15 - 18:00

After the wedding breakfast, join us downstairs for tea, coffee (or something stronger!) and a further surprise. We have private access to the patio outside so people can get some fresh air.

Evening guests arrive 18:00 - 19:00

Evening guests are invited to join the celebration in the Garden room from 6pm. As darkness falls, sparklers will be handed out to enjoy outside (a mini bonfire night celebration!).

PARTY TIME! 19:00 - 00:00

Both day and evening guests will be invited back upstairs to party the evening away! A DJ, white starlit dancefloor and fully stocked bar are sure to lead to a fun party evening. The downstairs area will remain open for the duration of the evening to act as a break out room for anyone wanting to escape the party. Our cake will be cut at 20:00 and evening food will be served at 21:00. Our evening food will be a variety of pizzas with garlic bread, wedges and salads. Our party will begin to wind down at midnight. Please ensure that any taxis/lifts are organised for this time.

By Car:

East Horton Golf Course has a large car park where all of our guests are allowed to park and leave their car overnight. Entering East Horton Golf Course in to your SatNav/Google Maps will accurately direct you to the venue.

Public Transport:

The nearest train station is Eastleigh. Eastleigh train station is a fairly large/busy station with trains often stopping there on journeys heading towards London, Southampton, Portsmouth etc. The station is a short taxi ride away from East Horton.

Buses are available from the surrounding areas. Visit the Bluestar and Stagecoach websites for routes and stops.

East Horton is only 15 minute drive away from Southampton Airport for those that want to travel in style.

Accommodation

We have a set number of rooms at the Marwell Hotel available at a discounted rate. The discounted rooms are first come first serve. To book, contact the hotel directly quoting our date and names.

Alternative accommodation is available in the surrounding area. Eastleigh has multiple hotels including a Travelodge and Premier Inn. Airbnb still has multiple accommodation options available for the 4th of November in the surrounding area.
